About Nepal Krishnamurti Jyotish Parisad

Dedicated to the study, practice, and advancement of astrological knowledge since 2010.

Our Mission

We are committed to preserving and advancing the ancient wisdom of astrology while making it accessible to the modern world. Our mission is to provide accurate astrological guidance, comprehensive education, and a supportive community for both practitioners and seekers.

We believe that astrology is a powerful tool for self-discovery, personal growth, and navigating life's challenges. By understanding the cosmic influences that shape our lives, we can make more informed choices and live more authentically.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate astrologers, our association began as a small study group meeting in Butwal, Nepal. As interest grew, we expanded our offerings to include formal classes, professional readings, and community events.

Over the years, we've grown to become one of the most respected astrological organizations in the region, known for our accuracy, integrity, and commitment to excellence. Today, we serve thousands of members worldwide while maintaining our roots in traditional astrological practices.

Our Core Values
